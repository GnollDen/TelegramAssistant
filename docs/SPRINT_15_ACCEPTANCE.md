# Sprint 15 Acceptance

## Purpose

Validate that Sprint 15 added a usable outcome and learning layer.

## Acceptance Checklist

## Outcome Coverage

- outcome record can be created
- strategy linkage exists
- draft linkage exists
- actual action linkage exists where seeded

## Learning Signals

- at least one learning signal is produced
- signals are grounded in recorded outcome data
- the system does not overclaim certainty

## Operational Usefulness

- outcome chain is inspectable
- the product can later support A/B and adaptation on top of this layer

## Verification

- build passes
- startup passes
- outcome smoke passes

## Hold Conditions

Hold Sprint 15 if any of these are true:

- no real linkage exists between strategy/draft/action/outcome
- matching is absent or non-functional
- learning signals are empty or purely placeholder
- persisted outcome chain is too weak to inspect

## Pass Condition

Sprint 15 passes if:

- the product now has a usable outcome/learning substrate for later optimization and experimentation
